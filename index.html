Welcome to East AI Club!

The East Chapel Hill High School AI Club is a computer science club where members learn Python programming and algorithms of AI to solve problems. The AI Club allows for students to gain experience in Python and AI, learn about recent breakthroughs in AI, and discover career opportunities in AI and computer science. No programming knowledge is required to join. Meetings will be held virtually.
